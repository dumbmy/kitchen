YiaYia's Greek Kitchen in Montague, NJ

About

YiaYia's Greek Kitchen is a Greek restaurant located on Route 206 in Montague, NJ. We serve everything from coffee to burgers to fries to smoothies, or authentic Greek dishes like gyro, spinach pie, moussaka, and many other delicacies. You can gather friends for a delicious dinner, or just grab a coffee in the morning. We serve only the best to our customers!
